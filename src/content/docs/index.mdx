## What is agent-fabric?

**Managed trust and connectivity for customer-owned AI.** Your agents, models and
services run on your own machines; agent-fabric gives them a private encrypted
mesh and a control plane that brokers keys, device identity and policy. The
control plane never sees your traffic — connectivity is end-to-end encrypted
(WireGuard), with relay fallback only for reachability.
